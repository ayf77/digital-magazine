ABOUT

Aymen is a gym leader in Hearthome City who specializes in fighting type pokemon. She's trained in various forms of martial arts and has a knack for battle strategy. Some people tend to think she's hot-headed due to the nature of her pokemon team and fighting skills, but in reality she's quite level headed and calm, especially when it comes to thinking strategically during battle. She also highly values sportsmanship. Aymen is good friends (and rivals) with Maylene, Vailstone City's fighting type gym leader, and often they like to spar for fun. She also trains not only pokemon, but other people in the way of martial arts. People also often come to her for help to train them before they go off to fight Cynthia, the Pokemon Champion of the Sinnoh League.


Aymen's favourite colours are white and blue. Her dark blue hair is always tied up in a bun, and she wears loose clothes, both of which are more efficient to have in combat. She wears a blue aura sphere neckalce that has been passed down in her family for generations. All the previous members of her family have had a Lucario as their main pokemon, hence the reason the heirloom has been passed down, to symbolize the bond that they share. She's trained her Lucario ever since it was just a little Riolu, and it is her most loyal friend, who never leaves her side during a battle.
