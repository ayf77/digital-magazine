ABOUT US

Erica

Ever since she was little, Erica has been entralled by the world of Pokemon; she was introduced to this magical world thanks to her parents who bought her Pokemon Pearl on the Nintendo DS. Since then, Erica has played more games from the series and expanded her interest to collecting Pokemon cards as well. Her favourite Pokemon are Giratina and Galarian Corsola.

Aymen

Aymen has been a fan of Pokemon ever since she was a little kid. From watching the show and movies, to playing the games on her Nintendo DS, to collecting the cards, she loved all things Pokemon. Becoming an editor for PokePost was one of the most exciting opportunities she's been able to have. Her favourite Pokemon are Lucario and Pikachu.

Mel

Mel's interest in Pokemon started in grade 3, when their entire class started trading with each other. Though their grade 3 cards got stolen out of her locker, Mel’s current collection can be pried from their dead hands. From that followed watching Indigo League with her brother, to finally playing their first pokemon game, Pokemon X. The Pokemon that hold the title of Mel's favourites are Bulbasaur and Glaceon.
