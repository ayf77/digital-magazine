ABOUT US

Welcome to PokePost !

Welcome to the wondrous world of Pokemon! PokePost is THE digital magazine to meet and learn about strong trainers in different regions all around the world. Every issue has a set of 3 different people we interview including but not limited to the well-respected archeologist and champion of Sinnoh–Cynthia, the elusive wanderer–Red, and even the popular fashionista–Elesa. We strive to deliver intriguing and informative news about each trainer’s origin, personality, and their beloved Pokemon. We also wish to give hopeful young trainers a form of inspiration and role models they can look up to while pursuing their Pokemon trainer path. However, we believe in keeping our selection of guests diverse; PokePost also looks to interview other careers such as Pokemon breeders, stylists, and even professors.



In celebration of our 100th issue, PokePost has set up special interviews with 3 powerful Elite Four members who have decided to take a break from Pokemon battles and travel around the world. Reo–the enigmatic and mischievous kitsune spirit who roams the streets of Stow-on-Side alongside her Hisuian Zoroark; Aymen–the headstrong but compassionate brawler accompanied by her faithful Lucario; and Mel–the quiet alpine forest ranger who takes care of the Frost Caverns assisted by her Abomasnow. Thank you to our loyal readers for supporting PokePost, please stay tuned for future issues and enjoy reading about the magical world of Pokemon!
