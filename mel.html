Mel

Age: 18

Region: Kalos

Specializes in: Ice Types

Location: Dendemille Town

Badge: Alpine Badge

About

Mel works as a Pokemon Ranger in the area surrounding the Frost Cavern. The Frost Cavern is located in the mountain range outside Dendemille town, hidden in a pine forest. Pokemon Rangers work to protect wild Pokemon, their environment and lost or hurt trainers. It is a popular place for trainers with Eevee's to go if they wish for a Glaceon. Which means there's always someone that needs help after getting lost in the cold. Trainers also have a tendency to upset the nearby Abomasnows, so Mel spends a good bit of time calming them down.


With the chilly weather, Mel wears winter gear to keep them warm. She has a thick white winter coat with green detailing around the cuffs, similar to a pine tree. Dark blue snowpants let Mel not have to worry about being thrown in the snow. It sometimes get windy up on the mountain, so Mel's hair stays up with green ties as to not blow around. It also lets them match with their ace, Abomasnow. This makes Mel dependenent on their big green and white scarf to protect her face and neck from the cold. Like other Pokemon Rangers, Mel also carries a rope and other tools that may help in the forest.
