REO

Age: 19

Region: Galar

Specializes in: Ghost Types

Location: Stow-on-Side City

Badge: Kitsune Badge

ABOUT

Reo is a mysterious fox spirit who enjoys running around the back alleys of Stow-on-Side pranking unsuspecting victims. Despite wearing a mask to hide herself while pranking others, she becomes more recognizable by her ornate kitsune mask. Reo is also constantly followed by two will-o-wisps named “Aka” and “Shiro”. One of her favorite pastimes is pranking Allister, another gym leader in Stow-on-Side as he has funny reactions. Allister used to run away the moment he noticed her presence, but recently has been warming up to her harmless antics.


Reo wears a white sleeveless hoodie with a fluffy collar that imitates the behavior of her wisps. The center of her hoodie has a large oval gem and the fabric has a crosshatch pattern that fades upwards. Her skirt is dark purple with a clay-coloured crosshatch design; her shin warmers are also the same clay colour and her left leg has a harness around it. Reo wears black chunky platform shoes to make a satisfying echoing sound when walking down empty alleyways (and not to make her taller). Her dark purple hair is put up in a high side ponytail with a white and red ribbon. She carries around an ornate kitsune mask and fox tail, supposedly from her past as a more traditional fox spirit. However, there are no records of her past appearance; be warned: if you try to dig up Reo's past you will be silenced by her wisps.
